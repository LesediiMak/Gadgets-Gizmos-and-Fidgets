About Gadgets and Fidgets

What we are about

At Gadget, Fidgets & Gizmos we offer you the latest tech products and gadgets, as well as selected products not yet readily available locally. Browse through our range of trending gadgets, shop online and have your items delivered to your door!

Lesedii Mak : CEO and Founder

Lesedii decided to venture into gadgets in 2014 when she finally accepted her love for Gadgets and Gizmos, finally accepting the labels "Nerd, Geek". She's poured her heart and soul into GFGs and would like to see the rest of the world tinkering with toys.
